Welcome to our SANDHATA an Academy of Art...
SANDHATA is an ingenious institution dedicated to Arts, starting its journey with a creative summer course on ‘Imaginative Art’ to reach close to the hearts of children. Here we provide guidance & assistance to children to co-ordinate their mind; eyes & hands simultaneously thereby improve their motor abilities. This course forms foundation to manifest their talent, skills and develop their self confidence.
Every child is gift of God having the ability to do something special, he will come across many pit falls. This takes more than 11 months of continuous efforts after birth to stand on their legs. Every step is a great achievement to the child, parents, grand parents etc. You are the role-model for them because every day they observe you, whatever you do they try to repeat them, do many mistakes. By doing mistakes, he will learn many things, more than doing the right things every time.
Why need art?
Today's education system is very competitive a, this needs the ability to think creatively and do things differently. There is little scope for many children in our country to get education which encompasses critical thinking, problem solving, decision making and creativity.
Art is the panacea for a child's true creativity that paves a road to all success. Art is something where a child can feel, touch, is able to see what he performed. He can measure himself, improves confidence level, gets mental stability, and makes him expressive. Art acts as a medium to the child & society.
